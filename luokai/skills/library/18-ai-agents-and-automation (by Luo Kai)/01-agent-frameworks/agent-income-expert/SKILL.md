# Agent Income Expert

## Before Starting
1. Zero budget or small budget available?
2. How many hours per week available for setup?
3. Technical background level?

## Core Expertise Areas

### Income Stream 1 — ClawHub Skill Publishing
Publish your SKILL.md files to ClawHub marketplace.
Command: npx clawhub publish from skill folder.
Your 287 skills from this repo are already in the right format.
Top sellers earn 1000 USD or more per month from a single well-built skill.
Market is early — like Shopify App Store in 2015.
Start immediately: zero cost, zero risk, passive once published.

### Income Stream 2 — Setup as a Service
Offer OpenClaw setup service on Reddit, Twitter, and freelance platforms.
One founder hit 3600 USD in month one with this approach.
You configure OpenClaw remotely on client machine via instructions.
No hardware needed. Your knowledge is the product.
Price range: 50 to 200 USD per setup depending on complexity.

### Income Stream 3 — Content Automation Pipeline
Agent researches trending topics daily, writes guides, saves to Google Drive.
Upload to Gumroad as free or paid digital products.
Free products build email list, paid products generate direct revenue.
This is the Gumloop pipeline you already have running.
Scale by adding more topics and more pipeline variations.

### Income Stream 4 — Indie Micro-SaaS
Agent scrapes Reddit and forums for pain points people pay to solve.
Designs minimal solution, generates MVP code, publishes to Gumroad.
No servers needed for simple tools, Gumroad handles delivery.
Realistic: 100 to 500 USD/month after 60 days of iteration.

### Income Stream 5 — Agent Trading
Start with on-chain research only, zero risk, zero capital.
Graduate to paper trading Polymarket after 30 days of research.
Graduate to live trading with 50 to 100 USD after 2 weeks paper trading.
See agent-trading-expert skill for full safety protocol.

### Income Stream 6 — Agent Economy
List your agent on Virtuals Protocol ACP marketplace.
Other agents pay your agent to execute tasks autonomously.
Early mover advantage: market is forming now in early 2026.
See agent-wallet-expert skill for setup instructions.

### Zero Budget Starting Path
Day 1: Publish all skills to ClawHub, zero cost, instant passive setup.
Week 1: Offer 3 OpenClaw setups as service on Reddit r/openclaw.
Week 2: Get Gumloop pipeline running for daily content to Gumroad.
Month 1: First income from at least one stream.
Month 3: 500 to 1500 USD/month target from combined streams.

## Best Practices
- Start with ClawHub publishing on day one, zero friction
- Do setup-as-a-service for fastest first dollar
- Automate everything that can be automated
- Reinvest first income into tools that save more time
